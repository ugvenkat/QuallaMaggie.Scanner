✅ 1. Stop‑Loss Market Order (Automatic Protection)
Robinhood does not support bracket orders, but it does support stop‑loss market orders, which are the simplest and safest way to protect your capital without watching the market.

How it works
After you buy a breakout

Identify the breakout day low

Place a stop‑loss market order at that price

Robinhood will automatically sell your shares if the price hits that level

Why this works for you
You do not need to monitor intraday

You do not need to check charts during work

You do not need bracket orders

You do not need mental stops

You do not need to react to sudden moves

This is the only fully automated stop‑loss method available on Robinhood.

Example
Breakout day low = $98.00  
Your stop = $97.50

You place

Code
Sell X shares
Stop Price 97.50
Order Type Stop Loss
If the stock hits $97.50 at any time, Robinhood sells automatically.

Why market stop (not limit stop)
A stop‑loss market order guarantees execution.
A stop‑loss limit order may not fill during a fast drop.

For your lifestyle, execution certainty is more important than perfect price.

✅ 2. End‑of‑Day Trailing Stop (QualMaggie Method)
QualMaggie does not use intraday trailing stops.
He uses end‑of‑day closes to manage trades.

This fits your lifestyle perfectly.

How it works
Every day at 355–400 PM CST, you check

Did the stock close below the 10‑day EMA

Did the stock close below the breakout day low

If yes → you sell manually.

Why this works for you
You only check once per day

Takes 10 seconds

No intraday monitoring

No stress

No screen time

Matches the actual QualMaggie rules

Why this is safe
End‑of‑day trailing stops avoid

intraday noise

stop hunting

unnecessary shakeouts

emotional decisions

This is how most professional swing traders operate.
